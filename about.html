About us

TailorBase is a modern cloud-based solution for custom tailors and ateliers. It allows you to instantly save client measurements, manage your database from any device, and guarantee data security thanks to Google authentication. Forget about paper journals—with TailorBase, your entire order history and precise body measurements are always at your fingertips, just a click away.
